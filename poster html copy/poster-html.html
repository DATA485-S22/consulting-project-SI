Introduction

What is Supplemental Instruction?

  • Supplemental Instruction (SI) is a student lead homework session, where the SI leader is a student who has already received an A in the course.
  • Students have diverse backgrounds, and some are not as prepared for college level material as others. SI aims bridge this gap, so every student has an equal opportunity at success.
